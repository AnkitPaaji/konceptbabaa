About Us

Success is never coincidental. It is hard work, diligence, perseverance, learning, studying, sacrificing and most of all, passion for accomplishing your goals and making your dream come true. 

I, Pushpender Khulbe (founder & director at konceptbabaa), am a results driven, customer centric professional with a variety of degrees and diplomas including but not limited to CMA, LLB, B.Com, M.Com. For over 7 years I have also been a proud faculty of ICAI with an extensive record of proven results. I believe in the power of modern technologies and try to find new and exciting ways to use them for teaching.

Holding an experience of over 7 years, I possess a strong track record of in improving test scores and teaching effectively. My students have always scored an average of 60%. Furthermore, basis my conviction I guarantee best results in minimum possible preparation time in a stress free environment.
